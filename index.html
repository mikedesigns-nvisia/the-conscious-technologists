Our Manifesto

Principles guiding our work with artificial intelligence and technology

01

AI as Mirror, Not Master

We develop AI systems that reflect human potential back to us, acting as partners in our evolution rather than replacements for human judgment and creativity.

02

Radical Transparency

All our AI systems are open about their limitations, biases, and decision-making processes. We reject black box solutions that obscure how conclusions are reached.

03

Conscious Data Stewardship

We treat data as sacred, honoring the human stories behind each data point. Our collection and use of data follows principles of consent, minimalism, and respect.

04

Distributed Wisdom

We decentralize AI development to prevent concentration of power, creating frameworks where many voices contribute to shaping our technological future.
